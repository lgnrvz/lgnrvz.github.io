To provide our services, specifically posting content to Facebook Pages, we may access the following via the Facebook Graph API:

  • Public Profile: Basic information like your name and profile ID.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data collected strictly for the following purposes:

We only store access tokens required to perform the posting actions. You can revoke our access at any time via your Facebook Business Integrations settings.
